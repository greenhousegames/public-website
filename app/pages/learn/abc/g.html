--- title: Learning | Gravity letter: g header: G is for Gravity subheader: Gravity is the acceleration in the world prev: f.html next: h.html content: Take a look at the three sprites in the game. The left sprite does not have gravity applied to it. The middle and right sprites have gravity applied to them.

Due to having no gravity, the left sprite does not move once placed in the game. This results in the object having no velocity or acceleration.

Due to having gravity applied to the world, the middle and right sprite increase in acceleration, causing them to fall to the ground. Once the objects hit the ground, the bounce defines whether or not the object will rebound off the ground. The middle sprite has no bounce and the right sprite has 100% bounce. --- {{> learn_abc}}
