--- title: Learning | Kill letter: k header: K is for Kill subheader: Kill refers to destroying objects prev: j.html next: l.html content: Take a look at the sprite in the game. The sprite is given a random velocity and has dragging enabled. If the sprite goes outside of the world bounds it will be killed. A timer will be used to revive the sprite 3 seconds after it is killed.

Killing a sprite is a way to remove the sprite from the game. This causes the sprite to be hidden and all interactions with the sprite are disabled. The sprite can be revived, in which cause it is made visible and can interact with the rest of the world. --- {{> learn_abc}}
