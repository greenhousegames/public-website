A is for Acceleration

Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity over time

In this example, the greenhouse icon is set to accelerate towards the pointer with a max acceleration of 100. Notice how the velocity increases over time as the icon accelerates towards the pointer. Once the icon passes the pointer, the icon begins to accelerate in the opposite direction. This causes the icon to slow down until it reaches a velocity of zero. Once the icon reaches zero velocity, it begins to move back towards the pointer.
