--- title: Learning | Acceleration letter: a header: A is for Acceleration subheader: Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity over time next: b.html content: Take a look at the two sprites in the game. Both sprites start out with the same velocity. The bottom sprite has zero acceleration and the top sprite has positive acceleration.

Due to not having any acceleration, the velocity of the bottom sprite does not change. This is similar to cruise control in a car. When cruise control is activated, the speed of the car stays constant. This results in the car having zero acceleration.

Due to having positive acceleration, the velocity of the top sprite increases over time. This is similar to a gas pedal in a car. When the gas pedal is pressed, the speed of the car increases. This results in the car having positive acceleration. --- {{> learn_abc}}
