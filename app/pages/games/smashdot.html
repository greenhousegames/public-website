Dots will appear randomly on the screen and grow over time. The dots can be collected by tapping them, but be careful, the dots will disappear if they collide with each other.

Classic Mode

Collect as many points as possible! The larger the dot, the more points awarded when collected.

Survival Mode

Keep the game alive! The game is over when any two dots collide.

Battle Mode

Compete in real-time against a live opponent! Dots for you and your opponent will appear on the screen at the same time. The same basic rules apply for the dots except that you cannot touch your opponent's dots. High score wins!
